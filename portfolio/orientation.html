MassArt Orientation 2015

Branding + Identity / Print / Apparel Graphics

The theme for MassArt's 2015 New Student Orientation was "Create Your Own Reality", based around reality TV. In order to design an identity for such a broad topic, I created an icon system based around all the shows that the orientation leaders were using for their groups' themes. Those icons were then used as design elements for T-Shirts, Brochures and fliers, and postcards.

Completed for Massachusetts College of Art and Design Orientation 2015
