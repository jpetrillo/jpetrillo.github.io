Normal: The Literary
Works of Miranda July

Print / Motion

Normal:The Literary Works of Miranda July is a book cover series for the writings of author/artist Miranda July.

The book covers consider the thematic connection between her writing; the struggle to define 'normal'. Each book is designed to look as plain as possible, with minimal color usage on the spine to differentiate the books from each other. The front contains text from a wikihow article, they're similar to her style of writing through specificity and intimacy of trite/obvious aspects of life. The front text is meant to trick the viewer into thinking it's an introduction; however, it's a 'how to' article that seems odd and unnecessary to look up (how to make friends, leave the house, talk to strangers) that thematically relates to each book.

I then designed animated versions of three covers that reveal the rest of the steps in the wikihow article.

Completed at Massachusetts College of Art and Design for Typography III
