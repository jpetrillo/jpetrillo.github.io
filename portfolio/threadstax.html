ThreadStax

Branding + Identity / Print

ThreadStax is a start-up company that produces an innovative product for folding and organizing clothes.

My main concern was that this product was dangerously close to becoming an "as seen on TV" product, so I decided the best way to combat that was to to position it as a classic household product (like a clothes hanger), with a Modern and timeless design to match. The logo was designed to behave with the edge of the composition to further emphasize the concept of organizing mess. A printed process book was then designed for the project.

Completed at Massachusetts College of Art and Design for Graphic Design II
