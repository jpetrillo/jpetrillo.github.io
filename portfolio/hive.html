The Hive

Branding + Identity

The Hive at DTX is a property management company with spaces in Downtown Crossing (DTX) Boston. The spaces are designed as collaborative workspaces for small start-ups, tech companies, and also retail, so they needed the identity to be bold, contemporary, and dynamic

The Hive emphasized they needed their logo to portray dynamism and animate well for their electronic signage, so I created a dynamic logo system, creating multiple variations of the logo to be used situationally that would also be able to be animated.

Completed at KHJ Brand Activation for The Hive at DTX
